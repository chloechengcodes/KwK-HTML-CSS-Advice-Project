ABOUT CAMP

Hello and welcome to our website. We are three scholars; Ilana, Chloe, and Aileen, from Kode with Klossy San FrancisKode made this to help future scholars prepare for their experience for camp.

What to Expect

Your 1st Day

  • Meeting a group of people passionate about learning code
  • Be in a collaborative enviorment
  • Good snacks!

ADVICE!

Here is some advice we have for you:

  • Don't freak out if/when Karlie FaceTimes or visits! While she is amazing, she's still a human being ;)
  • Have fun and don't be scared to learn something new or to put yourself out there!
  • Take notes! You will be learning A LOT in a very short amount of time, so it's important to write things down! We rccomed taking pictures of the note posters that your TA's will make and copying them down into a notebook during a break or when you get home.
  • GO TO SLEEP EARLY! Camp starts at 9 and you definitely don't want to be tired while at camp.
